# 📊 Instamart Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Instamart retail sales data and provide meaningful business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard helps stakeholders monitor sales performance, customer ratings, outlet performance, and product trends. It enables users to explore the data using interactive filters and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Compare sales across outlet sizes and locations.
- Track outlet establishment trends over time.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business intelligence.

## 📊 Business Insights

- Total sales reached **$1.20 Million**.
- Medium-sized outlets generated the highest revenue.
- Tier 3 outlets contributed the highest sales.
- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are among the best-selling categories.
- Customer ratings remained consistently high with an average rating of **4.0**.
